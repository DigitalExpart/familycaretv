## Long Description (4000 chars max)
**FamilyCare TV: Your Unified Health & Caregiving Companion**

Managing health for yourself or a loved one shouldn't be overwhelming. FamilyCare TV is the complete caregiving platform that seamlessly connects your mobile device directly to your living room TV via Roku. Whether you're tracking medications, managing doctors' appointments, or coordinating with family members, FamilyCare TV keeps everyone on the same page.

### 🌟 Key Features

**Unified Medication & Task Reminders**
Never miss a pill again. Schedule medications, daily routines, and vital checks on the mobile app, and receive beautiful, synchronized push notifications on both your phone and your Roku TV screen.

**Caregiver & Patient Ecosystem**
Easily manage profiles for patients, kids, and even pets. Invite family members to your network so everyone stays informed about appointments and care routines.

**Roku TV Integration**
Turn your television into a powerful health dashboard. Link your mobile app to the FamilyCare TV Roku channel to view live calendars, medication schedules, and receive on-screen alerts without picking up your phone.

**Smart AI Assistant**
Have a question about your schedule or need help managing your tasks? Our built-in, secure AI assistant is ready to help you organize your caregiving duties.

**Secure & Private**
Your health data is encrypted and securely stored. We prioritize your privacy and never sell your personal information.

*Disclaimer: FamilyCare TV is a management and organizational tool. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.*
