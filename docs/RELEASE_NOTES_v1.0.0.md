# FamilyCare TV - Release Notes v1.0.0

## 🚀 Major Features
- **Unified Health Ecosystem:** Manage medications, appointments, and daily tasks in one intuitive app.
- **Roku TV Integration:** Link your mobile app directly to your Roku TV to view live calendars, schedules, and notifications on the big screen.
- **Smart Notification Engine:** Reliable, synchronized push notifications ensure you never miss a pill or appointment.
- **AI Health Assistant:** Secure, built-in AI to help you manage your scheduling and general inquiries.
- **Family & Caregiver Sharing:** Invite family members to coordinate care seamlessly.

## ⚕️ Care Modules Included
- **Patients:** Track vitals, notes, and specific care routines.
- **Kids & Pets:** Dedicated modules to manage the whole family's schedules.
- **Books & Music:** Curated media libraries accessible via the Roku TV application.

## 🛠️ System & Stability
- Complete production readiness pass.
- Optimized backend for faster synchronization.
- Advanced subscription management via Stripe.
