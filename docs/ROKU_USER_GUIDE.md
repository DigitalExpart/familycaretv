# FamilyCare TV: Roku User Guide

## Welcome to FamilyCare TV
FamilyCare TV turns your television into a centralized family wellness dashboard. You can view tasks, medications, upcoming appointments, and daily inspiration right from your living room.

## Linking Your TV
1. Open the **FamilyCare TV** channel on your Roku.
2. You will see an 8-character linking code on the screen.
3. Open the **FamilyCare** mobile app on your phone.
4. Navigate to **Settings > Link TV**.
5. Enter the 8-character code shown on your TV.
6. Your TV will automatically update and load your family's dashboard.

## Navigating the App
- Use the **Up/Down/Left/Right** directional pad on your Roku remote to navigate through the menus.
- Press **OK** to select an item.
- Press **Back** to return to the previous screen.

### The Home Dashboard
The Home screen automatically refreshes to show you the most relevant information for today:
- **Daily Inspiration**: The Verse and Drawing of the day.
- **Quick Status**: See due tasks, upcoming appointments, and medication reminders.

### Live Notifications
If you leave the FamilyCare TV channel open, a small notification banner will slide in automatically when a new reminder (e.g., Medication time) is triggered by the system. It will dismiss automatically.

### The Screensaver
When you aren't using your Roku, the FamilyCare TV screensaver will display beautiful artwork, the Verse of the Day, and a scrolling ticker at the bottom with your upcoming reminders.
