# Google Play Submission Master Checklist

Use this checklist while navigating the Google Play Console during your final submission.

## App Content & Declarations
- [ ] **Privacy Policy:** URL provided.
- [ ] **Data Safety:** Form completed based on `DATA_SAFETY.md`.
- [ ] **Content Rating:** Questionnaire completed (Likely Everyone or Teen depending on medical classification).
- [ ] **Target Audience:** 18+ selected.
- [ ] **Ads Declaration:** App does NOT contain ads.
- [ ] **Health Apps:** Declared compliance with Health App policies.
- [ ] **Financial Features:** Declared subscriptions/Stripe integration.

## Store Listing Details
- [ ] **App Name:** FamilyCare TV
- [ ] **Short Description:** Entered (80 chars).
- [ ] **Full Description:** Entered (4000 chars).
- [ ] **App Icon:** Uploaded (512x512).
- [ ] **Feature Graphic:** Uploaded (1024x500).
- [ ] **Screenshots:** Phone screenshots uploaded (min 2).
- [ ] **Contact Details:** Support email and website provided.

## Release Management
- [ ] **Internal Testing:** Uploaded initial `.aab` to Internal Track and verified by internal testers.
- [ ] **Closed Testing (Optional):** Promoted to Closed Testing for wider QA.
- [ ] **Production Release:** Created Production Release, attached Release Notes (`RELEASE_NOTES_v1.0.0.md`), and rolled out to 100%.
